User Information

--------------------------

In this visual representation of population and migration, you can quickly see statistics per country by clicking on the map.

The default year is 2021 (will update to 2022, as soon as the UN population database finalizes migration data for 2022), but you can use the year drop down to select any year back to 2010. This selection will update the populated stats on the left hand table, but the map coloration will stay fixed on Jan 1, 2021 - Dec 31, 2021.

As more databases are added to this visual, more filter buttons will become available at the top.

We hope you enjoy this data visualization and see how we, as a human race, migrate around our planet.
